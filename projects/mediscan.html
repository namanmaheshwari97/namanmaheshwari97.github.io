ABOUT THE PROJECT

MediScan is an application which uses Augmented Reality and a strong recommendation system to suggest whether a medicine is applicable for the given ailment.
The App aims to reduce the prevalent problem of self medication in India where people in rural areas consume medicines without knowing about its side-effects.
The App also provides home remedies for people who do not wish to pursue allopathy.
